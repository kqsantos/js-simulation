Welcome to Tree of Life

This is a simulation about tree growth and seed distribution.

The tree goes through 5 stages before dying:

  • Seedling stage
  • Sprout stage
  • Sapling stage
  • Small tree stage
  • Mature tree stage

Once the tree has reached the Mature tree stage, it has a 45% chance of dropping a seed within 3 cells of it.

The seed drop occurs on a randomized interval (150 ticks +- 10%) from the tree's maturity until the tree dies.

The age of the tree is also randomized (1000 ticks +- 10%).

1 second = 60 ticks.
